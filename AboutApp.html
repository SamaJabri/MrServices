About App

Mr service is an ordering services application. Our aim is to transfer the level of relationship between the customer and their needs services to a technical and professional level. Services are available in the easiest way, at the time you want and with the highest quality. The application goals :
Complete the required service within the required time and efficiency Application features:
  • Our services are integrated and traceable (receipt of the order, implementation, after-sales support)
  • Fast and direct booking
  • Customer can specify the time and date of providing the service
  • Easy to use
  • Multiple languages support
  • Multiple payment methods
  • Customer can add multiple addresses
Four steps that separate you from providing the service :
  1. Sign in
  2. Service selection
  3. Specify the details (time - address - payment method ... etc.)
  4. Service order
